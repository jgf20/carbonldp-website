Innovation for the Next Web

In his 2009 TED talk, Tim Berners-Lee (a.k.a. The Father of the Web) urged us onward toward the complete vision for the World Wide Web. "It's called Linked Data," he said. "I want you to make it. I want you to demand it." We're working to achieve that vision by creating an innovation platform that can transform your enterprise and give you new advantages from advanced technology.

Standards Based

Carbon LDP is based on Semantic Web standards developed by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) with contributions from industry leaders such as IBM, Microsoft, and Oracle. Base22 is a member of the W3C and participated in the development of the specification for the Linked Data Platform (LDP). Carbon LDP is a fully compliant LDP server that will integrate and interoperate with industry tools from leading vendors.
